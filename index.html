About Me

I am Thoriq Nadaghoist Suharyadi, a graduate of the Information System Security program at Politeknik Negeri Bengkalis. My academic background and research have given me strong expertise in network security, website development, system design, and Linux-based server technologies. I also have hands-on experience in penetration testing, security analysis, and developing storage and hosting solutions using set-top box (STB) technology.

Through my projects and research, I have developed the ability to think analytically and work collaboratively to deliver secure and reliable information systems. In addition to technical expertise, I am highly proficient in Bahasa Indonesia, advanced in English for professional communication, and currently learning Japanese to expand cross-cultural skills.
